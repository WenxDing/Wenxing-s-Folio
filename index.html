Introduction

Hello! I'm Alex Ding, a passionate game developer with a diverse set of skills that I've acquired and honed over the years. I specialize in Maya modeling, and my expertise extends to creating breathtaking game environments and intricate character designs. My toolkit is extensive. I'm proficient with software like Maya, Mudbox, Unity, Substance Painter, and now, I've delved into the impressive world of Unreal Engine 5 development. This latest addition allows me to further enhance the realism and interactivity of my projects. But my capabilities aren't limited to just design. I also venture deep into the realm of programming. Fluent in C++, C#, Java, and Python, I possess the flexibility to craft intricate game mechanics, interactive features, and so much more. With experience in 3D animation, I've brought my game characters to life, bestowing upon them fluid movements and authentic emotions, ensuring a richer and more immersive experience for players. And to keep everything organized and collaborative, I'm well-versed in using GitHub for version control and team-based projects. On a more personal note, I am an avid gamer at heart. Overwatch stands out as my all-time favorite, constantly fueling my inspiration and passion for the ever-evolving gaming world. Through games, we experience a blend of emotions, stories, and challenges, and I am committed to capturing these elements in the games I develop. I'm always on the lookout for fresh ideas and collaborations. Whether it's discussing games, design, or the latest in development techniques, I'm all ears!
